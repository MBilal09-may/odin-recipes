Pizza

Description

This is a pizza recipe.This dish is also very famous.

This dish is famous in Italy.its also very easy to make.

Ingredients

  • Active dry yeast
  • Warm water
  • Sugar
  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Olive oil
  • Crushed tomato
  • tomato paste
  • Garlic
  • Dries oregano
  1. In a large bowl, combine the warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for 5–10 minutes until it becomes foamy. If it doesn't foam, the yeast is no longer active.
  2. Stir in the flour, salt, and olive oil. Mix until a shaggy dough forms.
  3. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead for 6–8 minutes, or until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  4. Punch the dough down to release the air. On a floured surface, use your hands or a rolling pin to stretch it into a 12-inch circle. Pinch the edges to create a raised crust.

To make sauce

  1. Combine all ingredients in a blender or food processor. Pulse until you reach your desired consistency.
  2. Taste and add more seasonings if needed. For a smoother sauce, you can run the blender longer.
To assemble the pizza
  1. Place a pizza stone or baking sheet in the oven and preheat to 500°F (or the highest setting) for at least 30 minutes.
  2. : Transfer the shaped dough to a pizza peel or parchment-lined baking sheet dusted with cornmeal. Spread a thin layer of sauce, leaving a 1-inch border. Top with half the mozzarella, then your pepperoni and other toppings, and finish with the rest of the cheese.
  3. Slide the pizza onto the preheated stone or baking sheet. Bake for 10–15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and browned.
  4. Remove from the oven and let cool slightly before slicing and serving.
